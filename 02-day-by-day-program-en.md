# Program – 10 Days (Indicative and Realistic)

- **Day 1**: Welcome, workshop tour, option choices, first assemblies
- **Day 2**: Wooden structure (RH/LH body), sanding, gluing, squaring, mechanical base
- **Day 3**: Keyboards & buttons – axes, springs, touch feel
- **Day 4**: RH/LH mechanics – complete assembly, height and travel adjustments
- **Day 5**: Bellows – making, fitting, adjustments, airtightness
- **Day 6**: Grills & finishing – mounting, decoration, wood finishing
- **Day 7**: Reeds – sorting, installation, initial tensioning, note-by-note tests
- **Day 8**: Fine adjustments – air leaks, mechanics, key alignment
- **Day 9**: Complete RH + LH tests, playing comfort, personalized corrections
- **Day 10**: Musical practice with guest musician, final handling, photos, group wrap-up

## Typical Daily Schedule
- 9:30–12:30: workshop building
- 12:30–14:00: lunch break
- 14:00–17:30: assembly, adjustments, individual guidance
- Evening: concerts, dances, friendly exchanges (according to schedule)
